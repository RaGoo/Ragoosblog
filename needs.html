Essential Human Needs

Fundamental requirements for survival, well-being and development

Physical Needs

Basic survival requirements including air, water, nutritious food, shelter, sleep, clothing, and protection from environmental threats.

Social Needs

The human requirement for love, belonging, acceptance, friendship, intimacy, and meaningful relationships with others.

Safety Needs

Personal security, financial security, health and well-being, and safety nets against accidents/illness and their adverse impacts.

Esteem Needs

The need for respect, self-esteem, status, recognition, strength, and freedom that builds confidence and personal worth.

Self-Actualization

Realizing personal potential, self-fulfillment, seeking personal growth and peak experiences through creative activities.

Cognitive Needs

The desire to know, understand, explore, learn, and make sense of the world through knowledge and meaning.

Understanding human needs helps create better societies, relationships, and personal development
